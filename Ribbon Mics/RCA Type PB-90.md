# RCA Type PB-90

**Polar pattern:** Bidirectional

**Freq. response:** 30 Hz to 6 kHz

### Notes
- General P.A. applications
- Wide frequency range (though not as wide as many other mics featured on this list)
- Lots of proximity effect
